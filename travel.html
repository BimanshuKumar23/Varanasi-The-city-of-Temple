Explore Varanasi

Varanasi, also known as Kashi or Banaras, is one of the oldest continuously inhabited cities in the world.
It is a major spiritual and cultural hub in India, situated on the banks of the sacred Ganges River.
Varanasi is renowned for its ghats (steps leading to the river), temples, music, and spiritual practices.
